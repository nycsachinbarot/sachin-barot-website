Ceremony

A shared recalibration.
A return to something we've lost.

Across cultures and centuries, communities have gathered in ceremony — to pray, to witness one another, to call in what they needed to move forward. Some traditions used plant medicines. Others used tobacco, chanting, prayer, or silence. What they shared was community: recalibrating, rebooting, and renewing together, rather than alone.

Modern life has stripped much of that away. Ceremony is an invitation back into it.

Held in community at one of our retreat locations, Ceremony is a single, powerful experience of collective presence — set within meditation, nature, and intentional space (set and setting), allowing recalibration to happen not in isolation, but witnessed.

Why community ceremony matters

Indigenous cultures around the world have long understood something modern life has forgotten: that resetting — recalibrating direction, rebooting energy, renewing purpose — often happens best in the presence of others who are walking the same road. Ceremony is a return to that — a space to be seen, to reset, and to move forward together. This isn't only about healing what's wounded. It's about recalibrating what's misaligned, and renewing what's gone quiet.

What to expect

  • Held in community, in nature, at one of our retreat locations
  • Grounded in meditation, intention, and set and setting
  • Offered as a standalone experience, or as an add-on to a full retreat

Some things can only be healed in the presence of others. This is a return to that.

The R.E.S.T.™ Protocol

REST™ Protocol

A guided four-week journey of reconnection, reflection, and transformation.

Insight alone doesn't create change. Transformation happens through awareness, intentional action, reflection, and practice.

The REST™ Protocol is a structured four-week container — one weekly 1:1 call, paired with daily practices — designed to help you slow down, reconnect with what matters most, and return to your life with greater clarity. Not to become someone new. To remember who you already are.

What's included

  • Four weekly 1:1 sessions, one per week across the full month
  • Daily guided meditation practices (30–60 minutes)
  • Reflective journaling prompts — self-inquiry, emotional awareness, relationships, purpose and direction
  • A morning awareness practice, before email or news, to shift from reaction to intention
  • Trigger mapping — identifying recurring patterns and how you'd like to respond instead
  • Nervous system restoration — breathwork and three weekly "stress recovery hours" with no agenda
  • Reconnecting with nature — earth, water, fire, air, woven through each week
  • A weekly reflection practice to track what's shifting

Who it's for

For those who know something needs to shift, but aren't ready — or don't want — to step into a group setting. This is where the work begins privately, at your own pace.

The goal is not perfection. The goal is presence.
