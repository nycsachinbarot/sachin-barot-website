Operator turned advisor, partnering with founders to scale companies, drive growth, and build meaningful businesses.

Who I Am

After exiting my company, I shifted my focus toward supporting early and growth-stage teams across the wellness ecosystem—helping them scale through strategic partnerships, go-to-market strategy, sales and marketing execution, and capital formation when needed.

My background spans building and scaling companies, developing revenue engines, and creating partnerships that unlock growth. Earlier in my career I helped scale a fintech company from ~20 people to over 550 employees before going through the founder journey myself.

The Journey
01

Capital Strategy & Financial Advisory

Supporting founders and leadership teams through capital formation, financial strategy, and growth planning. From early-stage raises to later-stage positioning, I help structure narratives, refine models, and connect companies with aligned investors — while building the financial discipline needed to scale.

02

Business Development, GTM & AI-Driven Growth

Driving revenue through focused go-to-market strategy, partnerships, and modern growth systems. I work with teams to build repeatable sales engines, activate strategic relationships, and integrate digital marketing with AI tools to improve targeting, conversion, and execution efficiency.

The Work Today

Now I work with people who are operating at a high level but know something isn’t fully aligned. Through retreats and ceremonies, I create spaces where people can step out of their normal environment and actually look at what’s going on beneath the surface.

Outside of that, I work one-on-one and with organizations — helping leaders build more awareness, better relationships, and more sustainable ways of operating. This isn’t about stepping away from ambition. It’s about bringing more clarity and stability into it.

Most of the people I work with don’t need more information. They need space, honesty, and the right environment to reconnect with themselves.
